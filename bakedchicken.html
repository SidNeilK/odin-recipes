Description: Simple Baked Chicken Breasts

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  • Four skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • Add moiture to chicken breasts
  • Add salt
  • Add flabor and heat with creole seasoning
  • Pour chicken broth onto baked chicken
