Description: Crispy Fried Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilt
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  • Cut four-pounded white chicken into piece
  • Add acidic buttermult tenderize the chicken
  • Add flour to add crispy
  • Add spice and seasoning
  • Vegetable oil for frying chicken
